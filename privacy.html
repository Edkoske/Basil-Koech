Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

This website supports the Basil Koech campaign for Member of County Assembly (MCA), Kedowa/Kimugul Ward, Kericho County, Kenya. We respect your privacy and handle personal information responsibly.

Information we collect

When you use the contact form, we collect the name, email address, and message you submit. We may also receive basic technical data such as browser type and approximate location from standard web server logs when you visit the site.

How we use information

Contact form submissions are used to respond to enquiries, coordinate campaign activities, and communicate with supporters in Kedowa/Kimugul Ward and Kericho County. We do not sell your personal information.

Third-party services

Form messages may be processed by FormSubmit to deliver email to the campaign team. External links (such as merchandise) are governed by the privacy policies of those third-party sites. Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts.

Data retention

We retain contact messages only as long as needed for campaign communication and record-keeping, unless a longer period is required by law.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting the campaign using the details on the main website.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through the contact form on the main site.
